Privacy Policy

At AI Photo Studio, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information and images.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Your email address and unique user ID when you create an account via Google or Email sign-in.
  • User Content: Photos you upload for background removal or editing.
  • Usage Data: Information about your subscription status (e.g., Free vs. Pro) and app usage.

2. How We Use Your Images

Your images are used solely for the purpose of providing the editing service (removing backgrounds, erasing objects, etc.).

  • Processing: Images are processed securely. We do not use your photos to train public AI models.
  • Storage: Processed images are saved to your private Gallery so you can download them later. You can delete your account to remove all data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to help run our service:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication, database storage, and file hosting.
  • Stripe: For processing subscription payments securely. We do not store your credit card details.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Access to your gallery is restricted to your specific user account via secure authentication rules.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@aiphotostudio.co.uk
